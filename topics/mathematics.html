Mathematics

“If people do not believe that mathematics is simple, it is only because they do not realize how complicated life is.” — John von Neumann, Mathematician and Computer Science Pioneer

Maths isn't as scary as you think if you are willing to learn, relearn and unlearn. Recommended study pattern is to learn the basics and learn the steps to solve a problem the long method then once you are comfortable with that pratice using the shortcut methods. Jumping directly to the shortcut way might not be sustainable.

From analysing your personal cash flow to understanding Large language models(LLMs), maths gonna help you. It's also a great way to exercise your brain.
